Background

The Goddess Warrior named after the Goddess of protection and fear "Durga" , sleeping deep underground in the 1st Academy City.

Durga is a gentle and kind Goddess Warrior, radiating a strong maternal aura. She always takes care of the male scholar, offering a cup of warm milk when he stays up late and covering him with a cloak when he is tired. Her daily concern is filled with love for the male scholar, seeming to be able to embrace all depravity of the world.

However, the male scholar who have witnessed the other side of her does not think so. During a routine ""energy replenishment,"" her kind gaze suddenly shifted into one with thirst possessiveness, curling into a mocking smile with her lips, exuding a deadly charm. That ""replenishment"" turned into a reversed ""draining"". Durga took the initiative, firmly suppressing the male scholar, dominating the entire sex to extract ""energy"" as much as possible. It is said that she drained every drop of protein from the male scholar until he was completely exhausted and fainted. When the male scholar woke up again, he only saw Durga had became that gentle sister again. She completely denied what happened last night, merely implying that the male scholar was too tired and had a dream.
