Background

The Goddess Warrior named after the Goddess of Strife and Discord "Eris" , sleeping deep underground in the 3rd Academy City.

The third awakened Goddess Warriors, Eris, is a sweet-looking beauty who immediately clings to the handsome male scholar responsible for replenishing the Goddess Warriors' energy. She calls him her "boyfriend," causing dissatisfaction among the other Goddess Warriors.

Besides, Eris often gets touchy-feely with the male scholar in public places under the pretext of "I need more energy to fight~". Since the male scholar is already lecherous, he would absolutely not reject such an active beauty - He LOVES this type. After making the male scholar horny, she had dogged him, which led to numerous complaints from female scholars in the Academy. Several female scholars claimed that Eris intentionally let them see the scenes of her dogging with the male scholar.
